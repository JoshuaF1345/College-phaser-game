How to Play

To move your character use the W A S D keys.

The objective of the game is to collect gummy bear sweets while dodging black holes.
To win you have to colllect 10 sweets without your Health points dropping to 0.

When you win your screen will display a message saying "You Win".
When your health points reaches zero you will lose. When you lose, the picture turns to a picture of earth split in half.

Resizing Issue

There is a resizing issue where the game shrinks when you resize the page This a problem with the Phaser Engine Press F5 to fix this problem when it is shrunk
